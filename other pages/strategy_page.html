1. Basic Information

Gamename:Guardians:Midnight BIT · Type：Action-Adventure RPG · Platform：Windows / Mac
Core Summary:

《Guardians:Midnight BIT》 is a game centered on map exploration and puzzle-solving, where players must unlock maps in order to discover the antidote that can save the school.

2. Quick Start

Beginner’s 5-Minute Guide — Do These Things First

Beginner’s Guide (Quick Walkthrough)

  1. Learn the basic key controls (WASD: Movement directions, Spacebar: Enter other buildings, B: Open backpack, P: Pause game, V: Save progress)
  2. Follow the story prompts to find relevant items and unlock different map dungeons.
  3. Complete mini-games to obtain gems, thereby unlocking the corresponding achievements.

Beginner Tips

  • Don’t rush to explore the entire map. First, get familiar with the dorm and campus overworld, learn the zombie patrol patterns, then try long-range exploration.
  • Prioritize obtaining key items. Get keys and antidote-related items first; they are essential for progressing the story and saving the campus.
  • Be cautious of zombies. Keep your distance and use buildings or obstacles to avoid them. If the countdown is triggered, focus on finding the antidote before side exploration.

Recommended Starting Route

Dorm Exploration → Obtain Dorm Supervisor’s Hint Items → Campus Overworld (Avoid Zombies, Collect Keys) → Enter Library/Teaching Building to Complete First Mini-Game

3. Characters and Equipment

4. Faqs

Q1: What should I do at the beginning?

You start the game in the dorm. First, talk to the dorm supervisor or other NPCs to get story clues and mission guidance. Interacting with NPCs is key to advancing the story and understanding your objectives.

Q2: Where can I get keys and items? What are they for?

Some campus buildings (library, classrooms, hospital, etc.) require keys to enter. Keys are usually found at the security booth. Items are used to trigger in-building story events or mini-games. Completing mini-games grants gem rewards, unlocks achievements, and progresses the story.

Q3: What should I do if I encounter a zombie? How does the countdown work?

Try to avoid zombies and keep a safe distance. If touched, a 25-minute countdown will start, during which you must find the antidote. Knowing the map, planning your route, and using items effectively are key to saving the campus.
